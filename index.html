Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Bunnyhop Games ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our mobile games (the "Games").

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our Games:

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, such as your device type, operating system, unique device identifier, IP address, and general location information.
  • Gameplay Information: We collect information about your gameplay activity, such as your game progress, level reached, time spent playing, and in-game purchases.
  • Crash Data: We collect information about crashes and errors that occur in the Games.
  • Information You Provide: We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your username (if applicable) and any information you provide through customer support channels.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the Games: We use the information we collect to operate and improve the Games, including troubleshooting, analyzing trends, and personalizing your experience.
  • To provide customer support: We use the information we collect to provide you with customer support.
  • To send you marketing communications: We may use the information we collect to send you marketing communications about our Games, with your consent (if required by law).
  • To comply with law: We may use the information we collect to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services in our Games:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track and analyze user traffic on the Games. Google Analytics uses cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your use of the Games, such as your device type, operating system, IP address, and general location information. This information is then aggregated and anonymized. You can learn more about Google Analytics and how to opt out of tracking here.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase to provide certain features in the Games, such as crash reporting and analytics. Firebase collects information about your device and gameplay activity. You can learn more about Firebase and its privacy practices here.
  • Game Analytics: We use game analytics services to track and analyze player behavior within the Games, which helps us understand how players interact with the Games and improve the overall gaming experience.
  • Aplovin: We use Aplovin to provide in-app advertising in the Games. Aplovin collects information about your device and gameplay activity to deliver targeted advertising. You can learn more about Aplovin and its privacy practices by visiting their website.

Data Storage and Transfer

We may store and transfer your information to countries outside of your country of residence. The data protection laws in these countries may vary from those in your country of residence. We take steps to ensure that your information is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your information:

  • Access: You may request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your information.
  • Restriction: You may request that we restrict the processing of your information.
  • Objection: You may object to the processing of your information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information below.
